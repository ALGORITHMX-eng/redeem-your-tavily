# UNMAPPED — Worldwide Skills Mapping

> Your skills are worth more than you think.

UNMAPPED is open infrastructure that connects young people's 
real skills to real economic opportunity — anywhere in the world.

## The Problem
Hundreds of millions of young people in LMICs like Amara 
(22, Accra, Ghana) have real skills but are invisible to 
the formal economy. No credentials. No matching infrastructure.

## What It Does
- Maps informal skills to ISCO-08 occupation taxonomy
- Shows automation risk by 2030 for your specific skills
- Surfaces real job opportunities with local currency wages
- Career Guide AI for personalised next steps
- Policy dashboard for NGOs, ministries and funders

## Data Sources
- ILO ILOSTAT — labor force & wage statistics
- World Bank WDI — development indicators  
- Tavily — live job market search
- ISCO-08 — international occupation taxonomy
